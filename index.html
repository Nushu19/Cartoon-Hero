Geralt of Rivia

About Geralt

Geralt of Rivia is a Witcher - a Mutant Human created
in order to protect regular citizens from Monsters.
Although he tries to stay out of politics and wider conflicts,
his destiny has other plans for him

Geralts Characteristics

  • He has superhuman strength.
  • He doesn't speak much.
  • He tries to avoid political conflict but always ends up taking the right side.
  • He is a brilliant fighter.
  • His weapon of choice is a Sword,
    but more often than not he has to improvise.
