Geralt of Rivia

About Geralt

Geralt of Rivia is a Witcher - a Mutant Human created in order to protect regular citizens from Monsters.

Geralts Characteristics

  1. He has superhuman strength
  2. He doesn't speak much
  3. He tries to avoid political conflict but always ends up taking the right side
